Everything you need to know about RevvRadar

General

RevvRadar is a map-based automotive community app by ReignVillage LLC. It lets you capture vehicles using AI-powered identification, discover local car shows and events, and connect with fellow enthusiasts in your area -- all in real time.

RevvRadar is being built for iOS (iPhone and iPad). Android is not currently planned but may be considered in the future based on demand.

ReignVillage LLC is an app studio based in Bellevue, Washington. We build mobile applications for dedicated communities, starting with automotive enthusiasts. RevvRadar is our flagship app.

Features

Point your camera at any vehicle and capture a photo. Our AI analyzes the image in real time to identify the make, model, year, and other details. It works best with clear, well-lit exterior shots, though it can also identify vehicles from partial or interior views.

The map is the heart of RevvRadar. It shows vehicle captures and car events happening near you right now. Think of it as a live feed of your local automotive scene, all pinned to a map you can explore.

Yes. RevvRadar features a built-in event discovery system. Browse upcoming car shows, meetups, Cars and Coffee events, and more -- all shown on the map with details like location, time, cost, and event type.

As you capture vehicles and participate in the community, you earn badges and achievements. These reward your activity and help you stand out as an active member of the local car scene.

Subscriptions & Pricing

Yes. The Free tier gives you 3 vehicle captures per day, plus unlimited additional captures by watching short rewarded video ads. You get full access to the map, events, and community features at no cost.

Pro ($4.99/month) gives you 20 captures per day, detailed vehicle specs (trim, MSRP, drivetrain, 0-60), custom theme colors, and a Pro badge. Premium ($9.99/month) bumps that to 40 captures per day and includes early access to new features. Both paid tiers have reduced ads.

All subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. To manage or cancel, go to your iPhone Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Refund requests must go through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. We cannot process refunds directly since Apple handles all billing. See our Terms of Service for full details.

Privacy & Data

We collect your account info (email, username), vehicle capture photos, approximate location data for map features, and standard usage analytics. We do not sell your data to third parties. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

Yes. You can set your profile to private, which hides all your captures from the community map, feed, and search. Private captures are only visible to you.

An approximate location is attached to each capture for map display. Residential locations are automatically hidden to protect privacy. You control location permissions through your iOS device settings and can disable them at any time.

Getting Started

Download RevvRadar from the App Store, then sign up with your email, Apple ID, or Google account. Choose a unique username and you are ready to go.

Camera access is required to capture vehicles. Location access enables the map and event discovery. Notifications are optional but recommended so you never miss trending activity or events near you.

Email us at support@revvradar.com for app support or hello@reignvillage.com for general inquiries. We typically respond within 24-48 business hours.
