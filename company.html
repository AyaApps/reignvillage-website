ReignVillage LLC

Building apps for passionate communities

We're an app studio focused on creating mobile experiences that bring niche communities together. Starting with automotive enthusiasts, we're building a portfolio of apps that help people connect over shared passions.

Our Mission

In a world of endless scrolling and passive consumption, we believe in the power of local discovery and real-world connections. Our mission is simple: Get people caring about what's happening around them again.

We build map-first, community-driven apps that transform passive interests into active engagement. Whether it's spotting rare cars, discovering local events, or connecting with fellow enthusiasts, our apps are designed to move people from their screens to their scenes.
